About

ReviewAid is an open-source AI-driven tool designed to streamline the full-text screening and data extraction phases of systematic reviews. It leverages advanced large language models to classify papers based on PICO criteria and extract custom data fields, drastically reducing the manual workload for researchers.

Why did I make this?

I built ReviewAid after spending countless hours staring at PDF screens, manually checking inclusion/exclusion criteria for hundreds of papers. It was tedious, prone to error, and burned me out. I needed an assistant that understood context, could handle messy PDF text, and gave me reproducible data I could export directly to Excel. ReviewAid is that assistant -fast, accurate, and completely free.

Note: Please do not replace manual done screening and data extraction completely with ReviewAid. The main aim of this tool is to use it as a 3rd person reference to minimise any errors done manually and to make the research as precise as possible.
